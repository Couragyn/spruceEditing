Rachel Blackie

I’ve been an ESL instructor since 2014 and began freelance proofreading and editing in 2020. Outside of work, you’ll usually find me reading, walking my dogs, or trying to learn a new language.

  • ESL Teacher since 2014, with a wide variety of experience:
    • English-language kindergarten teacher

    • ESL Teacher at language schools for teens and adults
    • Academic Coordinator at a language school
    • Online one-on-one ESL teacher
